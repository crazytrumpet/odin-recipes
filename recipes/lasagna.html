Lasagna

A homemade recepie from Grandma's kitchen. This quick bake lasagna is sure to be a fan favorite!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spahetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
