Fluffy French Toast

French Toast your Momma never made for you!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Slowly whisk milk into flour, then whisk in remaining ingredients
  2. Saturate bread slices in the batter
  3. Cook French toast on a lightly oiled griddle or pan until golden on both sides
